Tips

Start with HH EH L OW. Then try V OW D ER. For a more "machine" sound, raise jitter a little and shorten transition time. For clearer vowels, increase the master slightly and slow the rate scale.

Filter bands match the Bell Labs Voder patent (US 2,121,142): quasi-logarithmic spacing from 0–7500 Hz with increasing bandwidth. Pitch range 80–320 Hz covers male through child voices.
